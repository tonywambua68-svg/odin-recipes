Kenyan Chai

A rich and creamy tea made with milk, tea leaves, and sugar, commonly enjoyed in Kenyan homes for breakfast or in the evening.

ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • i cup water
  • 1 teaspoon tea leaves or 1 tea bag
  • sugar to taste

how to cook;

  1. Pour water and milk into a pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Add the tea leaves or tea bag and let it simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Add sugar, stir well, and remove from heat.
  4. Strain into a cup and serve hot.
